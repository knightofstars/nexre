Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your import/export journey with Nexre.

What does Nexre do?

Nexre connects buyers and suppliers across borders, making import and export simple, secure, and trustworthy. We help you find the right trade partners, manage deals, and grow globally.

How does Nexre make money?

We earn through a commission on successful trade deals. That means we only get paid when you win — ensuring full transparency and aligned interests.

Do I need any documents to start trading internationally?

Yes — international trade usually requires basic documentation like a business license, tax ID, and relevant trade permits. Don’t stress though — we’ll guide you through what’s needed based on your country and product.

Can Nexre verify buyers or sellers before I commit?

Absolutely. We offer optional verification services to reduce risk and build trust before you enter into any agreement.

Is Nexre only for Indian businesses?

No. While we are based in India, we work with global clients on both sides of the trade — whether you’re importing into or exporting from India.

What industries does Nexre specialize in?

We currently handle pharmaceuticals, agriculture, textiles, handicrafts, and light manufacturing. We’re also open to custom requests — just reach out.
